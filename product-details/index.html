Travelling alone can be problematic

Solo travel is not a sexy option. We should have a choice of choosing our own fun travelling partners. The problems of loneliness, local troubles, no local connect, losing touch with the place once you have travelled away, security are all valid problems.

Locals can be fun to hang out with

Matching travellers together is a great way to build security and fun in the idea of travelling.

Our pricing

Pricing: Our pricing currently covers the expenses borne by the local who travels with you. For e.g. if you intend to travel around in Bangalore, you have to bear only the travelling/eating costs that the local travelling with you has to bear.
